### INFORMATION FOR FRESH STUDENTS (UTME & DIRECT ENTRY)
**Resumption Requirements:**
All fresh students (UTME, Direct Entry, and Transfer) must resume with **four (4) photocopies** of the following documents:
1. UTME Result Slip.
2. O' Level Result(s) (WAEC/NECO/NABTEB).
3. Veritas University Admission Letter.
4. Birth Certificate or Evidence of Age.
5. Attestation Letter.
6. Sponsorship Letter.
7. Medical Fitness Report.
8. Evidence of State of Origin.
9. Original O' Level Certificate (if available).
10. JAMB Admission Letter.
11. Evidence of Change of Institution to Veritas University (for those who did not choose Veritas as their first choice).

**Important Note on JAMB:**
- Candidates who wrote UTME with "Awaiting Results" or did not choose Veritas University as their first choice must visit a JAMB CBT Center to do a **Change of Institution**.
- You must upload your O' Level results on the JAMB portal before resumption.

---

### TRANSFER STUDENTS REQUIREMENTS
In addition to the general requirements for fresh students, Transfer Students must provide:
1. Academic Transcript.
2. Evidence of Transfer Fee payment.
3. Regularization of Admission: Visit a JAMB CBT center to regularize admission.
4. Indemnity Form: Submit this to the Vice-Chancellor's office for signature.

---

### BURSARY CLEARANCE AND FORMS
**Printing Forms:**
Students are expected to print **2 copies** of the following:
1. Bursary Clearance Form (Click "Print Clearance Form" on portal).
2. Debt Recovery Credit Form (Click "Print Credit Form" on portal).

**Payment of Dues:**
To access the clearance card, students must pay at least the required minimum percentage of tuition fees AND the following dues:
- Faculty/Departmental Dues.
- SRA Dues (Student Representative Assembly).
- NFCS Dues.
- Medical Screening Test Fee (Mandatory for Freshers).

*Note: Payments should be completed in advance to avoid delays upon resumption.*
